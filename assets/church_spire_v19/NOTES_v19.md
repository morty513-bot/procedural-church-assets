## Chosen upgrade set
- Warmed limestone palette (regional Saint-Emilion cue) while preserving stylized low-poly shading.
- Added belfry cornice belt and lantern collar for clearer mass hierarchy.
- Added 4 corner pinnacles at belfry level to strengthen Gothic vertical rhythm.

## Why these changes
- Saint-Emilion churches read as warm limestone volumes with clear vertical articulation.
- Low-poly assets benefit from big-shape hierarchy (belt/collar) more than micro-detail.
- Repeated 4-way ornaments preserve coherence and avoid noisy asymmetry.
